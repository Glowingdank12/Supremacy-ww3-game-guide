      📘 Help Guide

      Beginner Rules
      • Build Arms Industry early. Economy wins long games.
      • Do not research random units. Pick a plan and specialize.
      • Do not fight two strong enemies at once.
      • Scout before attacking. Blind attacks get punished.
      Pro Rules
      • Air superiority gives map control and protects your strike aircraft.
      • Radar helps you see danger before it reaches your cities.
      • Artillery should hit and move. Do not let it get caught.
      • Use diplomacy to isolate one enemy at a time.
      Beast Mindset
      • If you are not expanding, you are falling behind.
      • If your economy is weak, your army will die later.
      • If you have no anti-air, enemy air will farm you.
      • If you are reacting instead of planning, you are already behind.
